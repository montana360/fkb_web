Nuhu Salifu

Nuhu Salifu is a Chartered Accountant and a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants Ghana with over twelve years’ experience in Accounting, Finance & Taxation.

Nuhu is the Finance and Administrative Manager at Quick Credit and Investment Microcredit limited.

Nuhu’s expertise are in designing and implementing accounting systems, preparing financial statements and annual budgets, maintaining efficient budgetary control system, instituting internal control procedures and efficient credit control mechanisms, working capital management, capital budgeting among others.

Nuhu has good working knowledge in QuickBooks (Accounting Software), Microsoft Office Suite.

He worked with Nandom Rural Bank limited as an Assistant Accountant from June, 2006 to March, 2008 after his national service with the Wa regional Hospital as an Accountant Trainee.

Nuhu also worked with Boateng and Darko Depot Contractors limited as Accounts Officer from April, 2008 to February, 2010. Prior to joining Quick Credit and Investment Microcredit limited, Nuhu had worked with Akate Farms & Trading Company limited as Accountant from September, 2010 to December, 2017.

He is a product of the Institute of chartered Accountants Ghana (ICAG) after graduating with B.Sc. Accounting (2nd Class Upper Honours) from the University of Education Winneba-Kumasi campus ( 2008 to 2010).

He has an HND Accounting (2nd Class Upper Honours) from the Tamale Polytechnic (2000 to 2003). He is also a product of Wa Senior High School (1997 to 1999) and Jindabo Junior High School (1994 to 1996)
